7 Emirates of UAE

Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi, the capital of the UAE, which is located about 170 kilometers south-west of Dubai. The Abu Dhabi is well-known for its lifestyle and it is the one of the best tourist spots in the UAE. Abu Dhabi is the largest and most populated of the seven emirates that make up the United Arab Emirates, with over 80% of its land mass and a population of over 1.6 million people. Abu Dhabi is rich with the natural resources. Abu Dhabi is the only Emirate to still own abundant quantities of petrol and gas. The Emirate of Abu Dhabi alone holds 10% of worldwide petrol resources and 5% of worldwide gas resources.

With its World-class infrastructures and fascinating attractions, Abu Dhabi is a city of rich heritage where you can experience and enjoy a variety of museums, mosques, islands, deserts, entertainment, and shopping. Abu Dhabi is the best place for the visitors from all corners of the world; it is mainly attracted by its culture and environment. People from a wide variety of nationalities are proud to call Abu Dhabi home and believe that our Emirate is the best place to live, work and visit.

Dubai

Dubai is situated on the Eastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, in the southwest corner of the Arabian Gulf and it is the second largest emirate of the U.A.E; it covers an area of 1,588.4 square miles. It is a known fact that the architecture here is popular worldwide for its modern touch and technological design. The city offers various beautiful landmarks which is a fine example of modern man-made architecture. In Dubai, with year-round sunshine, intriguing deserts, beautiful beaches, luxurious hotels and shopping malls, fascinating heritage attractions and a thriving business community, Dubai receives millions of leisure and business visitors each year from around the world.

Dubai is one of the best places to visit in the world. In Dubai, you get views of the modern skyline, comprehensive infrastructure, brilliant attractions, glamorous shopping malls, fine dining and hotels. The major attractions in Dubai are Dubai Creek, Jumeirah Mosque, Burj Al Arab (Photo Shoot), Atlantis the Palm, BurjKhalifa, Dubai Mall, Jumeirah Beach, Madinat Jumeirah, Sheikh Zayed Road.

Sharjah

Sharjah is the third largest emirate in the country. Sharjah is known as the cultural city of the United Arab Emirates and a leading place of education in the Middle East. It is located near to the city of Dubai. Sharjah covers approximately 2,600 square kilometers. The Emirate contributes to about 19% of the overall population of the UAE, with 85% of Sharjah's 946,000 residents living in Sharjah city. Sharjah is very nice, especially near the coast, and some beautiful Islamic architecture. Sharjah is very popular in the trading sector because it has one of the deepest harbors in the world. Sharjah with its elegant mosques and museums, have been tagged as UAE's cultural capital.

The Blue Souq is popular for incredible bargains. You can see exquisitely built mosques, public buildings, museums and King Faisal Mosque â€“ the largest mosque in the UAE. Sharjah is popular with the traditional markets, modern shopping malls, many heritage sites and museums, mosques, lagoons, and it is the place to experience the natural beauty of true Arabian deserts, mountains and seas.

Ajman

Ajman is the popular and smallest city which is located on the western coast of the UAE. Ajman is the great city for shopping and you can spend your leisure time with the warm sun, white sand beach and clear waters. In Ajman, Ajman Museum is a popular museum which is the eighteen century fort located in the heart of the city. Here we can see the ancient aircrafts and old weapons once used in the battle. Designer outlets and major brands are available in the Ajam popular Mall called City Center Mall.

It covers about 260 square kilometers. In Ajman the two cities, Mesfoot and AlMenameh which are more popular for the agricultural environment. This city maintains a unique place compare to the other emirates in UAE, mostly due to its small size which creates a more intimate feel than many of the large cities. Ajman combines both an ancient heritage and modern development. Ajman port becomes a place of ship maintenance workshop with the increase of ships movement and trading exchange. Ajman is booming in the sectors of infrastructure, trade activity and real estate.

Umm Al Quwain

Umm al-Quwain means "Mother of two powers" is one of the emirates in the United Arab Emirates. It is located in the north of United Arab Emirates. It covers an area of 800 square kilometres. It has a sandy island with dense mangrove thickets; the islands are separated from one another by narrow creeks. Umm-al-Quwain is the fastest growing place for extreme sports. The traditional occupation of this city has been fishing and dates cultivation. Umm Al-Quwain is filled with natural beauty and manmade tourist attractions, drawing many sightseers and tourists from bigger regions like Dubai.

Umm Al Quwain city has witnessed drastic developments over the last few years including construction of buildings, roads, parks and other. The city's attraction lies in its long clean beaches, lagoon and public horse riding stables.

Ras Al Khaimah

Ras Al Khaimah is one of the major emirates in the United Arab Emirates. This Emirate is filled with beautiful mountains, red sandy desert and lush green plains along with a series of creeks and lagoons. Ras Al Khaimah provides a variety of entertainment and relaxation facilities including Hotels & Resorts, international cuisines' restaurants and spas.

The city holds wide variety of activities like Watersports, Sailing Cruises, Desert camps, Golf courses and Mountain Adventures. The emirate of Ras Al Khaimah is the best ultimate outdoor experience for visitors. 64 kilometers of coast line with crystal clear water and white sandy beaches are a heaven for all sun-seekers.

Fujairah

Fujairah is the fifth largest emirate in the UAE and it is the youngest of the Emirates, only becoming independent from Sharjah in1952. Fujairah Life to enjoy the scenic beauty of nature. Fujairah is the place of the finest and charming beaches in the United Arab Emirates. Fujairah is the only Emirates that holds mostly mountainous, but all the other Emirates are widely covered by desert. Fujairah's Port is the World's third largest bunkering center. It is close to the Indian subcontinent with its large demand for petroleum products.

The city is rich with nature and man-made treasures in its various mountains, caves and archaeological sites, which will show you the rich Arab heritage. Fujairah is the jewel of Arabia covered by sandy beaches.

Bidya is popular with visitors because it is very old, twin-domed mosque, generally considered the oldest mosque in the United Arab Emirates.
